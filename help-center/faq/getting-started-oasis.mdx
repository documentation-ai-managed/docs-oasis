## Getting Started

OASIS helps you manage your product design and web development projects in one place.

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://oasis.harveydesign.io`
2. Create your first workspace for your team or project
3. Invite collaborators by email
4. Start a new project from your dashboard

## Need help?

Contact support anytime via the help button in the app.
