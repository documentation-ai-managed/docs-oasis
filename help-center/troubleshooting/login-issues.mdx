---
title: Can't Log Into OASIS
description: Steps to resolve login problems in OASIS.
---

## Symptoms

- Error: "Incorrect email or password"
- Login page refreshes with no message

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the OASIS login page. Check spam folders for the reset link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache and cookies">
    Try logging in again after clearing browser data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, email support@harveydesign.io with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
